- Cost: 3 markdown files; budget-friendly (Critic storyboard was the precedent and was budget-affordable).

- (a) (b) (d) (f) all gated per `AGENT_MASTER_PROMPT.md` section 6: (a)(b) need L3 prototype + reproduction + checkpoint download authorization; (d) needs a separate user decision after reviewer pass; (f) is Phase 2 work and premature at L2-portfolio + storyboard-portfolio-just-completed stage.
